# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

ChurchCore Academy contributors are expected to communicate professionally, review ideas on technical merit, and create a welcoming environment for people of different backgrounds, experience levels, identities, and perspectives.

Examples of constructive behavior include:

- giving specific, respectful feedback
- explaining tradeoffs and assumptions
- accepting correction without hostility
- protecting private and sensitive information
- focusing disagreement on the work rather than the person
- helping maintain a safe environment for students and institutions represented by the product

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, sexualized conduct, deliberate intimidation, publishing private information, or disrupting project collaboration.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, project communication, and public representation of the project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through GitHub. Do not publish sensitive details in a public issue.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, restrict participation, temporarily suspend access, or permanently ban participants when behavior violates this policy. Reports will be reviewed as confidentially and fairly as practical.

## Attribution

This policy is informed by the Contributor Covenant and common open-source community standards.
